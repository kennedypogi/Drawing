Welcome

To Kennedy Arts ^3^



I draw because I find great delight and fulfillment in the creative process. Each stroke of the pencil or brush allows me to express my feelings and thoughts in ways that words cannot always do. Drawing helps me relax and focus, therefore it's an important part of my daily routine. I am continually inspired by the variety and vivid stuff I see on TikTok. The platform provides me to a diverse range of artists and styles from around the world, inspiring fresh ideas and approaches that I implement into my own work. The originality and ingenuity demonstrated by other artists on TikTok inspire me to experiment and expand my artistic approach.
